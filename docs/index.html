Introduction and Abstract

Modern digital platforms offer extensive privacy controls, yet in practice, managing these settings can be complex. Users often struggle to locate privacy options buried deep within account menus, and even when found, the descriptions can be vague or difficult to interpret. As a result, users are frequently unaware of what data is being collected, how it is used, or how to meaningfully adjust their privacy preferences.

Problem

Our project begins with this problem: privacy controls exist, but accessibility and clarity remain significant problems.

Contribution

We propose an agentic chatbot interface that serves as a centralized privacy control center. By leveraging natural language processing, the chatbot can understand users' instructions about privacy settings preferences and change it for them. We also developed an interactive dashboard that visualizes privacy data, making it easier for users to understand and navigate their privacy preferences effectively.
